Privacy Policy

Wander & Bloom is a free photography garden map of the Netherlands, made by Marianne Eggink. This page explains what data is used on this site and how.

Google Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to measure visitor numbers and understand how people use the map — for example, which gardens are viewed most often. This only happens if you choose to accept cookies when visiting the site for the first time.

If you accept:

  • Anonymous usage data is collected (pages visited, time on site, general location by country/region)
  • No personal data is collected or sold
  • IP anonymisation is enabled
  • Data is processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy

If you decline, no analytics data is collected at all. You can change your choice at any time via the Manage cookies link in the footer.

Location data

The map includes an optional button to show your current location. This uses your browser's built-in location services.

  • Your browser will ask for permission before sharing your location
  • Your location is only used to display a marker on the map
  • It is never stored, sent to a server, or shared with anyone
  • It disappears as soon as you close or refresh the page

You can deny location access at any time in your browser settings.

Cookies and local storage

This site stores one item in your browser's local storage: your cookie preference (accepted or declined). This is used so the cookie notice does not appear on every visit. No tracking cookies are placed if you decline analytics.

Third-party services

  • Google Analytics — only if accepted. Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenStreetMap / CARTO — map tiles. CARTO Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — typography. Google Privacy Policy
  • Imgur — garden photos are hosted on Imgur

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are based in the EU, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data. As this site collects no personal data directly, there is nothing stored to access or delete. For data held by Google Analytics, you can opt out using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.
